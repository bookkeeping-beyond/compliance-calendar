Important Notes – Washington

  • Semiweekly federal payroll deposits: Wed–Fri paydays → deposit by following Wednesday; Sat–Tue → following Friday. If $100k+ in taxes accumulates on any day, deposit next business day and remain at least semiweekly through 2027.[web:16][web:91]
  • Washington excise tax: B&O/excise returns are generally due at the end of the month following each reporting period; check your assigned filing frequency with the WA Department of Revenue.[web:68][web:72]
  • Annual/biennial reports: WA LLCs and corporations file annual reports due by the last day of the formation anniversary month; verify on the WA Secretary of State site.[web:110][web:115]
  • IRA/SEP reminders: Traditional/Roth IRA contributions for 2025 close on Apr 15, 2026, while SEP contributions for 2025 can be made until the 2025 tax‑return due date, including extensions.[web:105][web:108][web:109][web:111][web:113][web:116]

Important Notes – New York

  • Quarterly NY sales tax: Standard ST‑100 filers use NY’s sales‑tax year: Q1 Dec–Feb (due Mar 20), Q2 Mar–May (due Jun 20), Q3 Jun–Aug (due Sep 20), Q4 Sep–Nov (due Dec 20).[web:68][web:70][web:71][web:72][web:74][web:75][web:62][web:79]

  • IRA/SEP reminders: 2025 IRA contributions close Apr 15, 2026; 2025 SEP contributions follow the 2025 return due date, including extensions.[web:105][web:108][web:109][web:111][web:113][web:116]
